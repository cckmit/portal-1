Instruction for adding a comment by email

  1. Receive an email notification from CRM.
  2. Respond to the letter. At the same time, the title:
    • From - must contain the address of the registered user on whose behalf the comment will be posted;
    • To - crm@protei.ru;
    • Subject - must contain the CRM № of the request
  3. Add a comment.
  4. Send an email.
